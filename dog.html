The Dog

  1. Dogs Can smell your emotions- They can detect changes in your scent that correspond to feelings like fear, stress, or happiness, thanks to their insanely powerful noses

  2. They have a "third eyelid" - Called the nictitating membrane, it helps protect their eyes and keep them moist, especially during sleep or when blinking

  3. Dogs can detect diseases - Some dogs can sniff out cancer, diabetes (blood sugar changes), and even COVID-19 with remarkable accuracy

  4. A Dog's nose print is unique - Just like human fingerprints, no two dog nose prints are alike. Some Kennels even use them for identification.

  5. They actually have sweat glands - but only in their paws! that's why you might see damp paw prints on a hot day.
